--- title: Piccolo-Pub date: 2009-07-07T12:00:00-04:00 ---

Welcome to my new publishing system, piccolo-pub. This is just a simple script the runs over the files that makes up this site and produces the final HTML output. This means that all the content is now static HTML and serves very fast. It also means that for the short-term comments will be disabled.

I am considering adding an on-the-glass commenting system, like Disqus or js-kit. If you have any experience with such systems please let me know in the comments... on FriendFeed.
