--- title: 24 is Star Trek date: 2009-07-19T12:00:00-04:00 ---

Lynne and I have been watching old seasons of 24 on DVD, a guilty pleasure at best, I kept getting this feeling that I'd seen this series before, but I couldn't quite figure it out, until I realized that 24 is Star Trek:

  • Jack Bauer is Captain Kirk
  • Chloe is Spock
  • CTU is the enterprise
  • Jack always leads the away missions.
  • The Federal Government is the Federation
  • Chloe can fix anything by reversing a matrix filter, just like any problem could be solved on Star Trek by reversing the transmitter array.
