--- title: ApacheCon 2003 date: 2003-09-20T12:00:00-04:00 ---

ApacheCon, the official conference of the Apache Software Foundation, will be held in Las Vegas this November 16-19, and you should seriously consider attending. There's an impressive list of speakers for this show and lot's of cool sessions.

And, as Ted Leung kindly put it, there will be a "bonus" this year, as I will be presenting on Extending HTTP Authentication and Mark Pilgrim will be presenting on the AtomAPI. I'm looking forward to meeting many of you in person, so go register today, because it gets more expensive the later you wait.
