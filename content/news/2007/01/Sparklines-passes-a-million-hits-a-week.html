--- title: Sparklines passes a million hits a week date: 2007-01-10T12:00:00-05:00 ---

This is a screen shot of my logfiles, recording here for all posterity the sparklines web service crossing the auspicious milestone of one million hits a week.

That's a web service, written in Python, running as a CGI application, under Apache, shelling out a million hits a week, on a shared host, and in no way even causing the server to breath hard.
