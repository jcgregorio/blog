--- title: Steve Gillmor Blogger and war correspondent date: 2006-02-21T12:00:00-05:00 ---

The syndication wars have re-erupted, like a recurring rash for which there's no cure. The only difference from the last time this occured is that now everyone has a blog, including journalists, and unsurprisingly the most shrill and vacuous post in the whole debate is from journalist Steve Gillmor.

Maybe the difference between a blogger and a journalist isn't that journalists have editors, but that they need them.
