--- title: Spring 2005 date: 2005-04-03T13:00:00-04:00 ---

It's definitely spring time here in the Carolinas. The Cherry tree has started to bloom. It sits on the north side of the house and is a little behind the rest of the cherry trees in the neighborhood.

Last summer's treatment of the front yard, including de-thatching, aerating, and slit seeding has paid off. The lawn survived the winter and has come back quite well this spring.

The Bradford Pear tree is doing good, though the flower clusters aren't as large as they have been in past years.

The Weeping Willow is going strong, doesn't look like the beating it took two years ago didn't do any permanent damage.
